About Me

Hi, my name is Anthony Dang and I am a recent graduate with a degree in Computer Science and Human Physiology.

I initially pursued a degree in human physiology, aspiring to become a physician assistant. However, after gaining experience in a hospital setting, I realized my true passion lay in working with technology and data analysis. Motivated by this discovery, I obtained a second bachelor's degree in computer science, where I immersed myself in the world of data analysis. I honed my skills in programming and statistical modeling, and actively engaged in real-world projects, collaborating with teams to solve complex problems. Today, I bring a unique blend of expertise in human physiology and computer science to my role as a data analyst, where I leverage data to uncover insights and drive transformative change.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me, and I hope to have the opportunity to work with you soon!
